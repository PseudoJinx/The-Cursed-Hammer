Vegetable Stew

This full bodied vegetable stew simmered over our indoor cooking fire will leave you fat and happy, Adventurer! See the list of our finests seasonal vegetables below and enjoy the stew with our house brewed ale for the full experience.

  • Celery
  • Barley
  • Potatoes
  • Spring Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spinach
  • Dark Stout
  • Cabbage

Make it at home!

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Roughly chop your celery, potatoes, onions, and cabbage
  3. Smash your garic to release the fragrance
  4. Combine in your pot over roaring fire
  5. Enjoy some dark stout as well as saving some for your stew
  6. Let simmer for as long as possible to reduce
  7. EAT!
